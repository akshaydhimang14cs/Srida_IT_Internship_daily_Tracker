## Key Learning Highlights

### Module 1: Risk Assessment
This module built a complete understanding of the risk assessment lifecycle. The learning covered assets, threats, vulnerabilities, likelihood, impact, risk scoring, risk appetite, risk treatment, and monitoring. It also introduced practical ideas such as threat modeling, vulnerability mapping, qualitative and quantitative risk analysis, and documentation through risk registers.

### Module 2: Compliance Audit Essentials
This module was started during the second part of the week and focused on the basics of compliance auditing. So far, it covered compliance audit definitions, audit planning, scope setting, audit program concepts, evidence collection methods, and introductory ISO 27001 compliance auditing. The module is still in progress.
